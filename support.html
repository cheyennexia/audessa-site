Audessa Support

Audessa is a Chrome extension that reads the current webpage article aloud with a floating text-to-speech player.

Contact

Email support: hello@audessa.app

Basic Troubleshooting

  • Use Audessa on a normal webpage, not a Chrome internal page such as chrome://extensions.
  • Open an article page and click the Audessa extension icon.
  • If audio does not start, click Play again or reload the page and try once more.
  • If an article is not detected correctly, try another article page with clear main content.
  • Voice availability depends on your browser and operating system.
